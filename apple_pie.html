Apple pie



My grandma always says: « There is no time for an apple pie»". So are you ready for a new recipe ?


Ingredients:

  • 1 pie dough
  • 6 apples
  • 30g of butter
  • 13g of vanilla sugar

Have all of the ingredients ? So let's go



  1. First step, peel 4 apples then cut them in cube
  2. In a saucepan bake the apple pieces with a cup of water. Once they are soft enought add the vanilla sugar
  3. Let the applesauce cool down. And preheat the oven
  4. Meanwhile, peel the 2 last apple and cut them in thin wedges
  5. Once the applesauce cool enought, spread it in the base of the pie dough, then place the apple pieces on the top by forming circles
  6. At least, put some butter on the pie, then bake the pie 30 minutes in the oven
  7. Enjoy !
